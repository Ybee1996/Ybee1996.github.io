Our story so far

25th July 2020, Evening

What a nice day it had been. Dinner with the family, after which Agnese had received a cute gift from Yusuf! Was this a sign? Well, Agnese had no idea what feelings hid beneath the surface of that gift, until... Yusuf invited her into his room to have a chat. THE chat. He told her exactly how he felt, and wanted to know whether she felt the same. She said: I'll tell you tomorrow. Then followed a sleepless night.
